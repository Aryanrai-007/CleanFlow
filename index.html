    About this Prototype

    This prototype demonstrates the CleanFlow flow without live GPS or external APIs. All data is local and simulated.

    • Driver: mark areas collected, start/stop simulated route
    • Citizen: subscribe to simulated alerts and see truck approach
    • Admin: view collected areas and simple analytics

    Usage for hackathon/demo

    1. Open Driver tab and press Start Auto Route
    2. Open Citizen and subscribe to your area
    3. As driver marks areas, Admin updates in real-time
